Hello there, I’m Frantz Drouillard! I’m a UX/UI designer with a background in graphic design. I have translated my visual communication problem solving skills into solving the problems of user experiences. I’ve worn a lot of hats  over the years from design drop shipping to production management and that’s due to my flexibility and adaptability. I’m a team player who’s always willing, ready, and able to learn more. I really enjoy the UI design side along with the creative process of brainstorming/sketching while applying different ideas to come up with a solution to a design problem. I think it's more fun when collaborating on a project because in a team more ideas can be bounced off one another and combined for a synergized result that is as amazing as it is unexpected.
